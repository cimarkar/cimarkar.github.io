Introduction

Molly's Bakery and Cafe

Local and organic neighborhood breakfast and lunch spot. Breads and pastries baked fresh daily with seasonal ingredients. Molly's mission is to create a community through delicious eats.

Target audience

This site will be used by both regulars and new customers. Locals and tourists who are interested in eating healthy and supporting local farms and businesses.

With seasonal dishes and baked goods the menu is always changing. Customers can use the site to stay up to date. The website will be used to find the store hours, location, and special events. The website can also be used to find contact information to place an order, schedule an event, or ask questions.

About Us/Contact

Molly's Bakery is located at 123 Marsh Street in San Luis Obispo.

We are open Tuesday-Sunday from 8:00am to 2:00pm

Call us at (707)-484-3358!

Email us at molly@mollysbakery.com!

Molly's bakery is built on family recipies. We meet with our growers and visit farmers markets to support local agriculture and find the freshest imgredients. We strive to be involved in our community and want to provide a comfortable place for neighbors to relax and socialize.
